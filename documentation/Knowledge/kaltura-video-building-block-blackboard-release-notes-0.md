        <td valign="top" width="435">
          <p style="text-align: left;">

          </p>
          
          <p>
            The Screen Recorder is accessible from My Media, Course Gallery and the Mashup Tool. The screen records include:

          </p>
        </td>
      </tr>
      
      <tr>

            Engagement reports for instructors
          </p>
        </td>
        
        <td valign="top" width="435">

Are the videos being watched to the end? Which students contribute media to the course and how does that media fare?
          </p>
        </td>
      </tr>

        <td valign="top" width="198">
          <p style="text-align: left;">
            Create new media from the Mashup Tool 
          </p>
        </td>
        
        <td valign="top" width="435">
          <p style="text-align: left;">
            Creating an announcement and you suddenly realize that you want to record yourself or upload media to be included in it? Now you can do it all from the same location. Simply launch the Kaltura Mashup from the text editor and upload new media or use media that was uploaded in the past. The same flow exists wherever you have access to the text editor: in assignments, tests, quizzes, forums, blogs, and more.
